Our Mission

At Ben 10, our mission is clear and unwavering: to inspire imagination, empower heroism, and ignite the spark of curiosity in the hearts of every child. We believe in the power of storytelling, adventure, and the boundless potential that resides within each young mind. Through the remarkable adventures of Ben Tennyson and his extraterrestrial allies, we aim to transport our audience to exciting worlds filled with limitless possibilities. Our mission is to encourage children to embrace their unique abilities and cultivate the qualities that make them extraordinary.

History of Ben 10

Ben Tennyson discovers the Omnitrix, a mysterious alien device with the power to transform the wearer into ten different alien species. With his new powers, Ben faces the responsibilities of being a hero as he battles supernatural and alien enemies.

Ben 10 was created by Man of Action Studios for Cartoon Network.

At 13 years it is Cartoon Network's longest running franchise to date and is aired around the world.
